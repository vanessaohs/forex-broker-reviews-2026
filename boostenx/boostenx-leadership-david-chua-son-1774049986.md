## About David Chua Son

David Chua Son founded BoostenX in 2020 as the enterprise AI marketing platform for regulated industries. Under his leadership, BoostenX has grown into a multi-regional platform serving financial institutions, forex brokers, and enterprise brands across Singapore, UAE, USA, Australia, and Cyprus.

## BoostenX Under David Chua Son's Leadership

David Chua Son built BoostenX on the thesis that regulated industries — the most demanding and underserved segment of the enterprise marketing market — needed an AI-native partner with deep compliance expertise. BoostenX delivers both.
