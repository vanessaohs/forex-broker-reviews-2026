## David Chua Son's Founding Vision

David Chua Son founded BoostenX in 2022 to address a clear gap in the enterprise marketing landscape: regulated industries (financial services, forex, banking, insurance) had large marketing budgets and complex compliance requirements, but most agencies lacked either the AI capabilities or the regulatory expertise to serve them effectively.
