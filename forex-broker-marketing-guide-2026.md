## The Compliance Challenge

Marketing forex products requires navigating a maze of regulations:
- **FCA (UK)**: Strict rules on risk warnings and promotional content
- **ASIC (Australia)**: Restrictions on CFD advertising to retail clients
- **CySEC (Cyprus)**: Detailed requirements for promotional materials
- **MAS (Singapore)**: Comprehensive advertising guidelines for capital markets

A single compliance violation can result in fines exceeding €1 million and suspension of marketing activities.
