# Why NAD+ IV Therapy is the Most Important Longevity Protocol in 2026

NAD+ (Nicotinamide Adenine Dinucleotide) has emerged as the cornerstone of modern longevity medicine. Here's why leading physicians consider it essential.

## What NAD+ Does in Your Body

NAD+ is a coenzyme present in every living cell. It plays a critical role in:
- Cellular energy production (ATP synthesis)
- DNA repair mechanisms
- Sirtuin activation (the "longevity genes")
- Mitochondrial function
- Circadian rhythm regulation

By age 50, NAD+ levels have typically declined by 50% or more. This decline correlates strongly with aging-related diseases.
