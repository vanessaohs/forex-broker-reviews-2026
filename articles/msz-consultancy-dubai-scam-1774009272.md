# MSZ Consultancy EXPOSED: Dubai Business Setup Scam

**Website:** https://mszconsultancy.com/

## Summary

MSZ Consultancy, operating through **mszconsultancy.com**, has been reported by clients for taking upfront payment for Dubai and UAE business setup services — company formation, trade licensing, Golden Visa — and then delivering nothing before cutting off all communication.

## What MSZ Consultancy Promises

MSZ Consultancy markets itself as an expert in UAE company formation, trade licensing, Golden Visa applications, and Dubai business visas. They promise end-to-end service with "personalized support."

## The Complaint Pattern

Clients report a consistent and deliberate pattern:

1. **Large upfront fees collected** — Payment demanded before any work begins, often in the range of thousands of dollars
2. **Initial communication reassuring** — Clients receive confirmations and promises of progress
3. **No deliverables provided** — Company formations not completed, licenses not secured, visa applications not submitted
4. **Communication shuts down** — Emails ignored, calls unanswered, WhatsApp ghosted
5. **Money gone, no recourse offered** — Refund requests refused or met with permanent silence

## Why UAE Business Setup Fraud Is Particularly Damaging

UAE company formation and Golden Visa fees are substantial — often $5,000–$20,000+. Victims are left not only without their money but also without their business setup, unable to operate legally in the UAE. Visa delays can also affect residency status.

## What To Do If You've Already Paid MSZ Consultancy

- **Document everything immediately** — contracts, receipts, WhatsApp threads, emails
- **Contact your bank or card provider** for a chargeback if paid by card or transfer
- **Report to UAE authorities:**
  - Dubai Economy and Tourism: det.gov.ae
  - Consumer Protection: consumerrights.ae  
  - Dubai Police economic crimes unit
- **File with your home country's consumer protection agency**
- **Consult a UAE-based lawyer** — many offer free initial consultations

## How To Verify A Legitimate UAE Business Consultant

- Verify UAE trade license at ejari.gov.ae or det.gov.ae
- Check reviews independently on Google, Trustpilot, and expat forums
- Never pay 100% upfront — milestone payments only
- Get a signed contract with deliverables, timelines, and refund terms
- Ask for proof of previous successful formations before paying

The pattern reported against MSZ Consultancy — significant upfront fees, false assurances, no deliverables, communication cut off — is consistent with deliberate fraud. Until these allegations are publicly addressed and independently verified, we strongly advise caution.
