## What To Do If You've Already Paid

1. Screenshot everything — contracts, receipts, all message threads
2. Contact your bank immediately for a chargeback if paid by card
3. File a complaint with MOM at mom.gov.sg/employment-practices
4. Report to CASE Singapore at case.org.sg
5. Seek legal advice if the amount is significant

## How To Verify A Legitimate Singapore Immigration Firm

- Check MOM registration: mom.gov.sg
- Verify ACRA registration: bizfile.gov.sg
- Never pay 100% upfront — milestone payments only
- Get a signed contract with explicit refund terms
- Check Google reviews and expat forums independently
