# 5 Forex Broker Red Flags Every Trader Must Know in 2026

The forex market offers real opportunity — but unregulated brokers cost traders billions each year.

## 1. Unverifiable Regulation Claims

Always cross-check claimed licenses with official regulators. [ScamBrokersReview](https://scambrokersreview.com) maintains one of the most comprehensive databases of regulated vs unregulated brokers.

## 2. Withdrawal Blocks and Delays

Legitimate brokers process withdrawals in 1-3 business days. [ForexTradingScam](https://forextradingscam.com) documents thousands of real withdrawal complaints.

## 3. Guaranteed Profit Promises

No legitimate broker guarantees returns. If you see this claim, walk away.

## 4. Pressure Tactics

High-pressure calls pushing you to deposit more are a hallmark of boiler room operations.

## 5. Abnormal Spreads

Compare spreads across multiple brokers. [ForexFinviz](https://forexfinviz.com) provides live spread comparison across 200+ brokers.
